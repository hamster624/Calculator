Notation Guide

E100#2#3#2 (hyper-e notation) represents E100^E100 which is the first step (E100#2). Then after you get the result you do result^result^result (E100#2#3) and then that result^that result.

If you see stuff like (#1^4) it just means #1#1#1#1.
